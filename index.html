Find me @

I am a Ph.D. candidate at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign and my research focuses on designing and building an organ-dedicated positron emission tomography (PET) system. During my research, I gradually realized the value of machine learning to medical imaging, and I developed a strong interest in programming and data analysis. In the future, I want to develop my career as a software engineer and pursue my passion for the intersection of AI and medical imaging.
