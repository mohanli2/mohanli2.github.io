Resume

I am a Ph.D. student in the Radiological Instrumentation Lab at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. My doctoral research focuses on building a high-resolution head-and-neck cancer dedicated positron emission tomography (PET) system. I have been going through the whole process of a system development including Monte Carlo simulation, detector assembly and characterization, system assembly and calibration and system performance evalution. I am also interested in the application of machine learning and parallel programming in image reconstruction and processing.
